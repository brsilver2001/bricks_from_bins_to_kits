About Ben's Bricks

Since 2015, our family has been discussing getting into the second-hand brick market.

All of us have been brick fans for years, and we each play with them in our own way.

Of our two children, one is more likely to turn a Batmobile and a Death Star into Spongebob's house than he is to return his bricks to their original kits.

The other, Ben, likes his bricks to be orderly, in complete kits, with directions.

Ben's Bricks supports the Chaos-Boy approach, but is dedicated to returning order from chaos. At this time, there is no way of returning mixed bins of bricks to their original kits without expert-level knowledge of brick ID numbers. Our app is the first step in training a computer to provide those numbers, in order to return a bin full of bricks to its original kits.
